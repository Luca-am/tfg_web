Abstract i paraules clau

  • AbstractThis article develops a semiotic account of brand performativity. It argues that brand performativity depends on citationality, the ways brand fragments are reanimated and recognized as citations, and analyzes how gaps between brand tokens, brand types, and brand ontology are calibrated through circulation and use.
